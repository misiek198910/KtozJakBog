<h5>Day 20. The Greatness of Saint Michael the Archangel in Love for Converted Sinners</h5>
<br>
Saint Michael the Archangel, burning with immense love for the human race, not only snatches the erring from the snares of sin, but becomes for them a wise guide, leader, and teacher on the path to holiness. With deep concern, he strives to ensure that the hearts of Christians are filled with noble virtues. A manifestation of this love was his help granted to our forefather Adam. Right after the fall of the first humans in paradise, the Archangel appeared to Adam and inspired his heart to sincere repentance. He taught him how to till the earth in toil, how to lead a god-fearing life, and what to do to attain salvation. He passed on to him the command of fidelity to the natural law and unfolded before him great mysteries regarding the future destiny of the world. He surrounded Eve with similar care. Thanks to this heavenly guidance, Adam left this world in deep intimacy with God, rich in merits and free from falling again. Who can fully comprehend such great and magnanimous love of Saint Michael?
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer</h5>
<br>
I have sinned against You, my God, forgetting Your infinite and unfathomable goodness. I beg for Your mercy, Lord! I would rather suffer death than ever audaciously turn away from You. And you, glorious Prince of love, Saint Michael the Archangel, be my shield, guide, and master, so that I may sincerely repent for my offenses and make amends for them to God. Intercede for me before the throne of the Highest Majesty and obtain for me the grace of a lasting and fruitful conversion of heart. Amen.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Greeting</h5>
<br>
Hail, Saint Michael the Archangel, who constantly obtain for the faithful servants the gifts of supernatural light and virtues. Enlighten the darkness of my soul.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Task</h5>
<br>
Stop today for a longer moment in silence and give yourself to deep meditation upon the most holy wounds of Jesus Christ Crucified. In spirit, kiss these wounds and make a firm resolution that you will no longer renew them with your sins.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer to the Guardian Angel</h5>
<br>
Angel of God, my guardian dear, to whom God's love commits me here. Ever this day be at my side, to light and guard, to rule and guide. Amen.

<h5>From September 20th we can begin the novena to Saint Michael.</h5>
<br>
<h5>Novena</h5>
<br>
Day One:
<br>
<br>
We beseech You, Lord, for the gift of Your great mercy. Deign to send to us Your faithful messenger, Archangel Michael, to carry our humble prayers before Your throne. May he himself instruct us on what gifts we are to beg You for, and may he intercede for us, ensuring Your fatherly care for the living and eternal rest in glory for the deceased. May the Lord of His grace send Saint Michael to constantly guard our souls and bodies.
<br>
Saint Michael the Archangel, powerful patron of our basilica, we unite with you in thanksgiving and praise of God for the gift of 550 years of presence and zealous ministry of the Pauline Order at Skałka in Kraków. We express our gratitude for our noble founder, Father Jan Długosz, and for all the benefactors who throughout the centuries supported the Fathers and Brothers in their holy mission. We humbly ask you that the fruits of this distinguished Jubilee Year may bring us all the grace of an authentic and zealous life according to the teachings of the Holy Gospel.
<br>
Through the powerful intercession of Saint Michael the Archangel and the heavenly choir of Seraphim, may the Lord make us worthy of perfect and pure love. Amen.

<h5>Pope Leo XIII Prayer to St. Michael the Archangel</h5>
<br>
Saint Michael the Archangel, defend us in battle, be our protection against the wickedness and snares of the devil; may God rebuke him, we humbly pray; and do thou, O Prince of the heavenly host, by the power of God, cast into hell Satan and all the evil spirits who prowl through the world seeking the ruin of souls. Amen.
