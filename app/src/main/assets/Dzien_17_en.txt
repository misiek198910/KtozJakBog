<h5>Day 17. The Greatness of Saint Michael the Archangel in Defending the Just Against the Powers of Darkness</h5>
<br>
Every just person who experiences harassment from evil spirits will find immediate rescue and victory as soon as they call upon the help of the Prince of Angels with deep faith. The history of the Church recalls the figure of Blessed Christina of the Holy Cross, who found herself facing a terrible onslaught from a powerful demon. Gripped by terror, she flew to the protection of Saint Michael, and his powerful intervention caused the powers of darkness to retreat immediately.
<br>
The same was true for Saint Mary Magdalene. When, in the solitude of her cave, she suddenly beheld a multitude of hellish monsters and a dragon ready to devour her, she turned in supplication to the heavenly Leader. Archangel Michael arrived to the rescue without delay, routing and irrevocably banishing the cruel beasts.
<br>
O how unfathomable is the power of Saint Michael and his love for souls seeking God! His very name strikes paralyzing fear into the abysses of hell and crushes all the snares of the enemy. Blessed be the Lord, who willed to so magnificently exalt His faithful Archangel!
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer</h5>
<br>
O invincible Leader of the heavenly hosts, who with ease crush the power of hellish forces! With all confidence, I call upon your powerful intercession in this constant, dramatic battle that is being waged for my immortal soul. Be the steadfast defender of my life at every stage, so that, shielded by your heavenly armor, I may safely reach the eternal glory prepared for the saved. Amen.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Greeting</h5>
<br>
Hail, Saint Michael the Archangel, armed with the flaming sword of justice, which utterly destroys the designs of evil spirits. Support me, so that I may never again succumb to the deceitful lies of the enemy.
