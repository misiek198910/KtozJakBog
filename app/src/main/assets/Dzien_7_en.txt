<h5>Day 7. The Greatness of Saint Michael the Archangel in Fervent Love for God</h5>
<br>
The unrivaled deeds of Saint Michael the Archangel are the most splendid proof of his boundless love and devotion toward the Creator. When at the dawn of history Lucifer raised a rebellion against the throne of the Most High, a time of great trial came for all heavenly beings. Seeing this audacious attempt to strip God of His due glory, Saint Michael—though previously subordinate to the power of the rebellious leader—ignited with holy wrath and seized his weapons. He stood courageously against the invader, proclaiming: The Lord is eternal, without beginning or end, while you, proud one, were called into existence only a moment ago! Who is like God? Only in Him shines boundless perfection and eternity untouched by the passage of time. He is flawless in His wisdom. You, however, Lucifer, along with your entire rebellious host, are merely fragile and limited creatures. How can you dare to equal yourselves with the Creator? Who is like God? He is all-powerful, infinitely good, and free from any blemish. He is perfectly just, and at the same time full of mercy. Have you raised your hand against the Almighty? Did you truly believe that you could defeat Someone who has no equal?
<br>
Just as Moses once called upon the sons of Levi, so the Archangel addressed the heavenly hosts: Whoever stands on the side of the Lord, let him follow me! Drawn by this call, the vast majority of the angels united around him, thanks to which they together routed the forces of darkness and cast them out of heaven. The glory of God was saved, peace was restored in paradise, and the Creator received the highest homage from the rescued hosts of pure spirits. How powerful and victorious this love proved to be!
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer</h5>
<br>
My God and Creator, I confess with contrition that I have so often admired Your creatures, forgetting about You—the Giver of all goodness. Lord, whose mercy knows no bounds and who desires my conversion, stretch out Your gracious hand over me and grant me forgiveness. From now on, I desire to direct my every thought and desire toward Your glory. And you, most splendid Seraph of heaven, Saint Michael, intercede for me before the Divine Majesty and ignite the fire of the purest love in my heart. Teach me to turn my eyes away from the fleeting vanities of this world, so that I may be able to love the eternal and infinite Goodness with all my heart. Amen.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Greeting</h5>
<br>
I greet you, Saint Michael the Archangel, who shine at the very head of the glorious choir of Seraphim.
<br>

<h5>Task</h5>
<br>
During the coming day, at hourly intervals, awaken in your heart a short aspiration: "God, Lord of my life, You are my only and supreme Good."

<h5>Prayer to the Guardian Angel</h5>
