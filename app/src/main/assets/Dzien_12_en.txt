<h5>Day 12. The Greatness of Saint Michael the Archangel in the Angelic Apostolate</h5>
<br>
Saint Michael the Archangel became a source of light and wisdom for the entire heavenly army. When the rebellious Lucifer attempted to deceive the pure spirits and draw them to the side of rebellion—which unfortunately succeeded in the case of some of them—he deluded them with the false conviction that the power and perfection of their own nature would be enough to achieve the fullness of happiness without regard for the will of the Creator.
<br>
At that dramatic moment, Saint Michael, uttering his monumental cry: "Who is like God!", revealed to the angels the truth about their creation. He reminded them that their entire existence comes from the Lord and only He is worthy of the highest praise and gratitude. Thanks to this, they understood deeply that attaining eternal happiness and beholding the Divine Countenance is impossible without relying on supernatural grace. This apostolic admonition from Saint Michael proved so effective that countless hosts of glorious spirits united before the throne of the Most High in an act of perpetual adoration. The testimony and teaching of the Archangel confirmed the angels in their fidelity, causing them to remain and continue in undisturbed joy forever.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer</h5>
<br>
Incomparable Teacher of Heaven, Saint Michael the Archangel! With all my heart I praise and bless the Lord for filling you with such great wisdom, so that you could enlighten, strengthen, and save the angelic hierarchies from eternal ruin. I beg you, deign with the assistance of my personal Guardian Angel to disperse the darkness of my mind and heart, so that I may walk the path of God's commandments without straying and with full devotion. Amen.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Greeting</h5>
<br>
Hail, Saint Michael the Archangel, most wise Guide and Doctor of the angelic hosts. Enlighten my soul with Divine light in every minute of my life.
