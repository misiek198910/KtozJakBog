<h5>Day 18. The Greatness of Saint Michael the Archangel in Love for Sinners</h5>
<br>
Unrivaled is the immensity of love that Saint Michael the Archangel harbors for every human being. It is precisely this fervent affection that prompts him to constantly hasten to the aid of those who have gone astray, to help them break free from vices and return to the bosom of Divine mercy. In the biblical Book of Genesis, we find the story of Hagar, who, having fallen into conflict with her mistress, Sarah, was forced to leave the house of Abraham along with her son Ishmael. As she wandered helplessly through the wilderness, the Angel of the Lord appeared to her, directing a call to her: "Hagar, slave of Sarai, where have you come from and where are you going? Return to your mistress and submit to her authority."
<br>
In a mystical sense, Hagar symbolizes someone foreign, a lost wanderer in this world. Sarah, on the other hand, meaning "princess," represents God Himself—the Sole Ruler from whom man unwisely distances himself every time he succumbs to base desires and tramples upon the holy commandments. The sinner, fleeing from the Creator, enters the dry and inhospitable desert of the world, where they will not find a single drop of spiritual consolation.
<br>
It is then that Saint Michael, acting as God's messenger, cries out with power to the conscience of the erring person: Where are you coming from and where are you heading? Open your eyes and see from Whom you are fleeing! You are turning away from God, who is the source of all goodness! What will be the end of this dangerous path? Return without delay to your Creator, who is the center of your existence and the foundation of eternal joy! Submit to His love and begin to faithfully fulfill His holy will.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer</h5>
<br>
To you, most gracious Prince of love, Saint Michael the Archangel, I fly with all my misery and sins. Trusting in your powerful intercession, I beg for Divine light that will allow me to fully know and understand my offenses. Pour into my hardened heart the gift of sincere sorrow and deep contrition, so that I may be able to reject all evil with hatred. You, who care so much for the salvation of man, lead me by the hand with the assistance of my Guardian Angel, so that I may enter anew upon the path of virtues, love God with all my heart, and safely reach the gates of heaven. Amen.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Greeting</h5>
<br>
Hail, Saint Michael the Archangel, merciful Rescue and Hope of people entangled in sins. Come to my aid and grant that my stagnant soul may come alive once more through God's grace.
<br>
