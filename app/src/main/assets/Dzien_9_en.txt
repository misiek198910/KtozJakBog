<h5>Day 9. The Greatness of Saint Michael the Archangel in Love for the Mother of God</h5>
<br>
From the very beginning of his existence, Saint Michael burned with a deep love not only for the Holy Trinity, but also for the Most Blessed Virgin Mary. God revealed to the pure spirits the image of the future Mother of the Incarnate Word when unfolding the mystery of redemption. The proud Lucifer swelled with pride and refused to pay homage to the Woman, deeming that her human nature stood lower than his own powerful essence. At that moment, Saint Michael the Archangel arose to courageously stand in defense of the dignity of the future Mother of God. He cast the rebellious leader out of heaven and, at the head of the faithful angels, worshipped with deep reverence her who was to bring the Savior into the world.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer</h5>
<br>
Saint Michael the Archangel, most perfect devotee of the Immaculate Virgin! With admiration I look upon your devotion to the Queen of Heaven, and I beg you for the grace of a true, filial devotion to Her in my heart. You, who readily served the Mother of God, obtain for me at Her throne the grace to be numbered among the ranks of Her faithful servants. Through your intercession, together with the angelic choirs, may I gain the eternal joy of beholding God and praising the Most Holy Virgin Mary throughout all ages. Amen.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Greeting</h5>
<br>
Hail, Saint Michael the Archangel, steadfast defender of the dignity and throne of the Most Holy Virgin Mary.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Task</h5>
<br>
Turn your thoughts to the Queen of all angelic choirs and recite the Hail Mary prayer nine times, followed by the Glory Be three times, giving thanks for the powerful intercession of Saint Michael the Archangel.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer to the Guardian Angel</h5>
<br>
Angel of God, my guardian dear, to whom God's love commits me here. Ever this day be at my side, to light and guard, to rule and guide. Amen.
