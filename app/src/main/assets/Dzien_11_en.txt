<h5>Day 11. The Greatness of Saint Michael the Archangel in Love for the Angels</h5>
<br>
The unfathomable love with which Saint Michael the Archangel surrounded the other heavenly spirits earned him the honorable title of the Father of Angels in the tradition of the Church. Saint Jerome explains that those spiritual beings who lead others and surround them with a protective wing are rightly called fathers. Archangel Michael, as the Prince of princes and the most powerful of heavenly leaders, holds authority over all nine choirs, while he himself pays the deepest homage to God—the only Father of all creation.
<br>
During the great trial in the heavens, the Archangel defended God's honor with utmost firmness, caring for the salvation of the angels entrusted to him. He nourished their hearts with spiritual goodness and, with fatherly love, protected them from the venom of pride and rebellion, which became the ruin of the fallen spirits. For this reason, the entire heavenly host holds him in boundless respect, venerating him as their wise guide and father.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer</h5>
<br>
O most zealous Apostle of Heaven, invincible Saint Michael the Archangel! For the sake of your holy fervor, with which you saved angels and men from eternal ruin, obtain for me at the throne of the Most High a sincere desire for eternal goods. Ignite within me the will to assist my neighbors on the path to sanctification, so that by my life and example I may draw others to God and, together with them, enjoy the beholding of the Divine Countenance in heaven. Amen.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Greeting</h5>
<br>
I greet you, Saint Michael the Archangel, powerful Commander of the Heavenly Army. Deign to direct my every step and lead me by a safe path.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Task</h5>
<br>
Think today of someone in your surroundings who has distanced themselves from God or neglected the sacramental life. Make a discreet attempt at a sincere conversation to bring this person closer to Divine love and encourage them to return to confession and the Eucharist.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer to the Guardian Angel</h5>
<br>
Angel of God, my guardian dear, to whom God's love commits me here. Ever this day be at my side, to light and guard, to rule and guide. Amen.
