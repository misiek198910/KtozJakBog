<h5>Day 1. The Unsurpassed Splendor of the Nature of Saint Michael the Archangel</h5>
<br>
When contemplating the perfection of the angels, they are often compared to luminous morning stars, which are the masterpieces of God's creation. Among these countless heavenly hosts, it is Saint Michael the Archangel who shines with the most powerful and purest light. As the most perfect of creatures, he occupies a unique, honorable place in paradise, surpassing all other heavenly spirits in his nature.
<br>
His essence is so permeated with holiness and purity that he dwells in the immediate proximity of the throne of the Most Holy Trinity. While other angels cover their faces in trembling before the Majesty of God, Michael stands before Him with unwavering confidence and courage. In his case, the title of Archangel does not merely signify belonging to one of the lower choirs, but rather emphasizes his absolute leadership and natural majesty. O glorious heavenly Leader, how great is your glory!
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer</h5>
<br>
Glorious Prince of Heaven, Saint Michael, from the bottom of my heart I rejoice in your power and perfection with which the Creator has endowed you. I thank the Holy Trinity for appointing you as the leader of the angelic hosts. I approach you with humility, aware of my weaknesses and how often I forget the dignity of being a child of God. I place myself under your reliable protection. Obtain for me the grace of clarity of mind, so that I may always remember to be grateful to God and faithfully serve Him every day. Amen.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Greeting</h5>
<br>
Hail, Saint Michael the Archangel, the brightest of the lights of heaven. May your splendor constantly disperse the darkness of my soul.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Task</h5>
<br>
Kneel in concentration before the image of the Archangel and recite three times the prayers: Our Father, Hail Mary, and Glory Be. Awaken in your heart thanksgiving to the Most Holy Trinity for the gift of such a powerful protector. Then, ask for the grace to wisely use the talents you have received from the Creator.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer to the Guardian Angel</h5>
<br>
Angel of God, my guardian dear, to whom God's love commits me here. Ever this day be at my side, to light and guard, to rule and guide. Amen.
