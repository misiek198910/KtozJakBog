<h5>Day 8. The Greatness of Saint Michael the Archangel in Devotion toward Jesus Christ</h5>
<br>
When the Savior of the world was born on earth, Saint Michael the Archangel, together with the heavenly hosts, hastened to Bethlehem to bow with deep reverence before the Incarnate Word. It was he who announced the joyful news of the Redeemer's coming to the poor shepherds, and with a wondrous star marked the path leading the Magi from the East to the Bethlehem stable. Then, watching over the safety of the Child, he brought to Saint Joseph in a dream the command for an immediate flight to Egypt, thereby saving Jesus from the cruel wrath of King Herod.
<br>
Countless are the manifestations of protection and support that Archangel Michael provided to the Holy Family during the hardships of exile. When the danger had passed and those who sought the Child's life had died, it was he who called Joseph to return to the land of Israel. When Christ finished His forty-day fast in the desert and rejected the subtle temptation of Satan, Saint Michael arrived with other angels to serve Him with love. The tradition of the Church also sees in him that strengthening angel who descended to Jesus when He was immersed in bloody prayer in the Garden of Gethsemane. He then brought to the Son of God the assurance that although all of heaven was interceding for Him, asking for the cup of bitterness to be removed, the salvation of mankind and the will of the Father could only be fulfilled through the sacrifice of the Cross and the shedding of the Most Precious Blood.
<br>
Saint Michael faithfully accompanied the Savior in the hour of His painful passion, and after His death, reverently escorted His holy soul to Limbo to announce redemption to the just. It was he who rolled back the mighty stone from the tomb on Easter morning, and on the day of the glorious Ascension, led the triumphant heavenly army to meet the King of Ages returning to glory.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer</h5>
<br>
O Saint Michael the Archangel, most faithful Guardian and defender of the earthly mysteries of our Lord! With admiration and reverence, I recall your constant presence with Christ. I beg you, ignite in my cold heart an authentic, living, and steadfast love for my Divine Redeemer. Grant that I may faithfully imitate His teachings on earth and, through your powerful intercession, may one day praise Him together with you in the kingdom of eternal light. Amen.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Greeting</h5>
<br>
Hail, Saint Michael the Archangel, most noble defender of the heavenly throne and the earthly path of Jesus Christ.
<br>

Go today for personal adoration of Jesus hidden in the Most Blessed Sacrament. If you have the opportunity, receive Holy Communion with a pure heart and spend a longer moment in silence meditating on the boundless love of God for man.
