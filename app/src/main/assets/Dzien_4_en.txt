<h5>Day 4. The Greatness of Saint Michael the Archangel in Heavenly Glory</h5>
<br>
Unrivaled devotion and deep humility paved the way for Saint Michael the Archangel to the highest glory in the heavenly kingdom. His love for the Creator ignited more fiercely than in all other angelic hosts, and he was the first to offer God a total gift of his pure essence. This boundless love brought forth deeds of unimaginable perfection, which elevated him to the peaks of the heavenly hierarchy. Since the measure of eternal reward and praise corresponds to the strength of love, Archangel Michael, by loving God most perfectly, enjoys a glory inaccessible to other spirits—a glory so powerful that the tradition of the Church sees in him the closest confidant of the Most Holy Trinity and the monarch of heavenly majesty.
<br>
Christ Himself reminds us on the pages of the Gospel that everyone who humbles himself will be exalted. These salutary words find a perfect reflection in the attitude of Saint Michael. It was precisely with the weapon of humility that he challenged the proud Lucifer. Aware of his own nothingness before the infinite Majesty, he uttered the famous cry: "Who is like God!", drawing millions of pure spirits with him into obedience and fidelity. In return for this humiliation, the just God entrusted to him the throne lost by the rebellious leader of darkness and appointed him commander of all angelic choirs. No human mind is able to comprehend the brightness of his countenance, the dignity, and the majesty with which he radiates in heaven.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer</h5>
<br>
O most noble Prince of Heaven, Saint Michael the Archangel! With deep reverence and awe, I praise you and bless the Lord for such a great exaltation of your holy person. Deign not to reject me, a wretched sinner, who falls with humility and trust before your countenance. Look upon my heart, full of contrition and shame because of countless downfalls, through which I have so thoughtlessly closed my path to eternal happiness.
Most splendid Protector, intercede for me with the merciful God! Revive in me a steadfast hope for the total forgiveness of my faults, strengthen my will in the faithful fulfillment of God's commandments, so that with your help I may one day look upon God face to face in His kingdom of glory. Amen.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Greeting</h5>
<br>
Hail, most noble Leader of the heavenly hosts, Saint Michael. Remember us in your powerful prayers and constantly intercede for us before the throne of the Most High.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Task</h5>
<br>
Recite three times the hymn in honor of the Most Holy Trinity, praising God for appointing Saint Michael as the defender of the faith and exalting him above all three heavenly hierarchies. Meditate for a moment on the value of Christian humility.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer to the Guardian Angel</h5>
<br>
Angel of God, my guardian dear, to whom God's love commits me here. Ever this day be at my side, to light and guard, to rule and guide. Amen.
