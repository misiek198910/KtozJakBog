<h5>Day 23. The Greatness of Saint Michael the Archangel as the Defender of the Holy Church</h5>
<br>
Saint Michael the Archangel manifests himself in the history of salvation as the most splendid and first defender of the Immaculate Virgin and the Catholic Church. He has declared an uncompromising war against Satan, who is a fierce enemy of God and the Mystical Body of Christ. This demon is the powerful dragon written of by Saint John on the pages of the Apocalypse. He desires to strike at the faithful in order to rob the Creator of His due glory, take away the love for Jesus from mankind, and close before them the path of rescue. However—as Daniel already prophesied—the Church has not been left without defense, for a powerful guardian, Saint Michael the Archangel, watches over it.
<br>
The evil spirit strikes at the Church in four different ways. The first battle is waged through bloody tyrants and persecutors. In these moments, Saint Michael courageously shields the faithful, confirming them in the steadfastness of confessing the faith, pouring peace into their hearts during torments, and multiplying their strength when, humanly speaking, hope is already lacking. The blood shed by the martyrs—as Tertullian aptly noted—becomes a fertile seed that strengthens Christianity. The second onslaught came from heretics and false teachings. Archangel Michael, by enlightening the minds of the Holy Doctors and assisting them during the deliberations of synods and councils, helped reveal the full brilliance and truth of the Catholic faith. The third war takes place within—through lukewarm and insincere Christians, who by their sinful lives stain the pure garment of the Church, which is the Bride of Christ. Saint Michael, by awakening consciences and renewing the desire for virtue in human hearts, makes the Church holy once anew and radiant with God's glory. The fourth, final battle will take place in the times of the Antichrist. Also at that dramatic moment, Saint Michael will stand guard over the flock of Jesus Christ, to ultimately strike down and defeat the deceiver.
<br>

<h5>Prayer</h5>
<br>
O most glorious Prince and undaunted Archangel Michael, commander of the heavenly hosts, powerful vanquisher of the hellish forces, and most faithful Guardian of the Church! Deign to cast off the chains and release from all torments all those who with full confidence fly to your wings. Obtain for us by your powerful intercession an abundance of supernatural graces, so that with a pure and fervent heart we may faithfully serve God every day of our lives. Amen.
<br>

Hail, Saint Michael the Archangel, heavenly and unshakable pillar of the Holy Church.

<h5>Novena</h5>
<br>
Day Four:
<br>
<br>
We beseech You, Lord, for the gift of Your great mercy. Deign to send to us Your faithful messenger, Archangel Michael, to carry our humble prayers before Your throne. May he himself instruct us on what gifts we are to beg You for, and may he intercede for us, ensuring Your fatherly care for the living and eternal rest in glory for the deceased. May the Lord of His grace send Saint Michael to constantly guard our souls and bodies.
<br>
Saint Michael the Archangel, powerful patron of our basilica, we unite with you in thanksgiving and praise of God for the gift of 550 years of presence and zealous ministry of the Pauline Order at Skałka in Kraków. We express our gratitude for our noble founder, Father Jan Długosz, and for all the benefactors who throughout the centuries supported the Fathers and Brothers in their holy mission. We humbly ask you that the fruits of this distinguished Jubilee Year may bring us all the grace of an authentic and zealous life according to the teachings of the Holy Gospel.
<br>
Through the powerful intercession of Saint Michael the Archangel and the heavenly choir of Dominions, may the Lord grant us the grace of full dominion over our senses and efficient protection against evil and disordered desires. Amen.
