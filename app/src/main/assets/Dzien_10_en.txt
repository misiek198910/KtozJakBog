<h5>Day 10. The Greatness of Saint Michael the Archangel in Love for Mankind</h5>
<br>
God in His essence is pure love, and everyone who burns with this holy fire becomes like the Creator. Saint Michael the Archangel, dwelling closest to the Divine Majesty among all heavenly spirits, is filled with this virtue to the highest degree. Just as God Himself surrounds man with infinite care, so too Archangel Michael, of all the angels, has most loved and embraced the entire human race.
<br>
The tradition of the Church constantly portrays him as a powerful defender and advocate of humanity before the throne of the Most High, zealously pleading for mercy and the forgiveness of our sins. His powerful intercession never remains without an echo—on the contrary—everyone who calls upon his help with confidence experiences an abundance of supernatural graces and is safely guided by him toward the gates of heaven. O how wondrous is the love of Saint Michael! Seeing human confusion and weaknesses, he hastens to us with an almost maternal tenderness and never ceases in his prayers.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer</h5>
<br>
O gracious and kind Prince of the angelic hosts, Saint Michael! I, a wretched sinner, fly with full confidence to your powerful protection, begging for the rescue of my immortal soul. Be my advocate and guide through all the days of my earthly pilgrimage, and above all at the hour of my death, obtaining for me the strength to repel all attacks of evil. Support me in this final battle. Amen.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Greeting</h5>
<br>
Hail, Saint Michael the Archangel, my reliable shield and rescue in every moment of affliction and need.
<br>

<h5>Task</h5>

Notice someone in need around you today and support them with sincere alms or a selfless act of mercy.
