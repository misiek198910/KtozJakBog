<h5>Day 2. The Greatness of Saint Michael the Archangel in the Gifts of Grace</h5>
<br>
Unfathomable is the immensity of grace with which the Lord of the universe adorned Saint Michael the Archangel, making him the most perfect spirit after the fall of the rebellious angels. Since this endowment corresponded to his powerful nature, no human being is able to comprehend the fullness of perfection of this extraordinary Protector. He was filled with virtues surpassing all other creatures: steadfast faith, boundless hope, a love so fervent that it ignites the entire heavens, and a profound humility that ultimately shattered the pride of Lucifer.
<br>
Saint Michael represents the most splendid model of holiness and a pure mirror of God's beauty, reflecting the perfection of the Creator Himself. As his faithful devotee, rejoice in these spiritual riches with which your Patron shines, and surround him with the sincerest love of your heart.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer</h5>
<br>
O, powerful Saint Michael the Archangel! The sight of the boundless grace that you draw unceasingly from the Divine source fills my heart with joy, yet at the same time makes me aware of my own misery and how unwisely I have wasted the gift of sanctifying grace. I deeply regret that despite repeatedly experiencing God's forgiveness and mercy, I have wounded the Heart of my Lord anew with my downfalls. I beg you, great Prince of the heavenly hosts, intercede for me at the throne of the Most High. Obtain for me the grace of true, deep contrition for my sins, the strength to fight temptations, and the gift of final perseverance in goodness. Amen.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Greeting</h5>
<br>
I greet you, Saint Michael the Archangel, exalted above all the angelic choirs and shining with the fullness of heavenly glory. You, who stand at the head of God's army, intercede for me in every minute of my life.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Task</h5>
<br>
During the course of this day, recite the Act of Contrition three times, begging the Most Holy Trinity to renew and strengthen the Divine life within you, especially if you carry the burden of sin in your heart. Make an intention to approach the Sacrament of Penance and Reconciliation.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Act of Contrition</h5>
<br>
O my God, I am heartily sorry for having offended Thee, and I detest all my sins because of Thy just punishments, but most of all because they offend Thee, my God, Who art all good and deserving of all my love. I firmly resolve, with the help of Thy grace, to sin no more and to avoid the near occasions of sin. Amen.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer to the Guardian Angel</h5>
<br>
Angel of God, my guardian dear, to whom God's love commits me here. Ever this day be at my side, to light and guard, to rule and guide. Amen.
