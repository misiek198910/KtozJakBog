<h5>Day 3. The Greatness of Saint Michael the Archangel in Cooperation with Grace</h5>
<br>
Consider that the value of even a single gift of Divine grace surpasses the riches of the entire created world. A person who approaches it with indifference risks an unspeakable loss. It was precisely this attitude that marked the abyss between Saint Michael and the rebellious Lucifer. The latter, in the blink of an eye, rejected the supernatural help that was meant to lead him to perfection and the healing of his will. On the other hand, Archangel Michael united himself with all his inspiration to the will of the Creator, turning the received gift to His highest glory.
<br>
Divine grace manifests itself as a holy movement of the conscience, light for the intellect, and pure inspiration of the will. It is not given once and for all—it comes in fleeting moments that become a priceless treasure for anyone who can accept and utilize them. How tragic is the position of someone who allows these moments to pass fruitlessly! Recall how many such callings God has directed to you, desiring to detach your heart from earthly vanities and direct it toward eternity. How often these salutary inspirations were ignored by you! Every wasted moment of Divine presence casts a shadow on our eternity, for no one is certain if the same gift will be offered to us once again. If you lacked fervor in the past, make a firm resolution now that from now on you will listen to the voice of God with full attention. Ask Saint Michael to obtain for you the grace of faithful and fruitful cooperation with every Divine movement.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer</h5>
<br>
O Saint Michael the Archangel, most faithful servant of the Lord, who with perfect wisdom accepted and multiplied the entrusted gifts of grace! I pay you homage and praise the infinite Goodness of God, which enabled you to heroically utter that solemn "Yes" to the Divine Majesty. Aware of my past negligence on the path to salvation and my frequent closing of eyes to the signs of Divine mercy, I fly to your powerful defense. Heavenly Prince, I beg you for help in overcoming my spiritual laziness and all weaknesses. Intercede for me at the throne of the Most High, so that I may respond to every inspiration with deep fidelity and thereby deserve to share in the eternal joy of the saved. Amen.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Greeting</h5>
<br>
I greet you, Saint Michael the Archangel, who with steadfast courage stood up to fight the darkness and pride of Lucifer. Deign not to reject my humble prayers and intercede for me, a sinful human being, with the Merciful God, so that I may praise your courage without end.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Task</h5>
<br>
Approach the image of Jesus Crucified with deep reverence, kiss His holy wounds three times, and say from the heart the words: "My beloved Jesus, I beg You, make it so that Your saving passion and shed blood do not prove in vain for me."
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer to the Guardian Angel</h5>
<br>
Angel of God, my guardian dear, to whom God's love commits me here. Ever this day be at my side, to light and guard, to rule and guide. Amen.
