<h5>Day 22. The Greatness of Saint Michael the Archangel as a Powerful Intercessor with God</h5>
<br>
The intercession of the angelic Prince is characterized by extraordinary effectiveness and supernatural power. A perfect proof of this is the biblical fate of the Israelites, who for long decades struggled with the Babylonian captivity, losing almost all hope of regaining their freedom. Although Archangel Gabriel, appointed as the guardian of that nation, constantly brought fervent supplications before the Divine Majesty, asking for an end to their national misfortune, the situation did not change.
<br>
In the face of these difficulties, Saint Michael decided to unite his powerful prayers with the cry of Gabriel. He presented to the Lord the immense suffering and humiliation that the oppressed people had been experiencing for over seventy years. His intercession was immediately heard, and the nation received the longed-for promise of liberation.
<br>
How great is the power of his advocacy! Archangel Michael obtains at the throne of the Most High gifts that other heavenly spirits were unable to secure. Entrust all your worries to him with full confidence, imitating the wisdom of the Church, and your requests will be answered.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer</h5>
<br>
O most illustrious and glorious Prince, Saint Michael the Archangel! I fly to your powerful protection, acknowledging in you my most faithful mediator and powerful advocate. Deign to remember me and constantly present my petitions to Jesus Christ, the Son of God. I deeply believe that when you take up my cause, my humble prayers will reach God's Heart, through which I will gain strengthening in earthly hardships and eternal joy in the heavens. Amen.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Greeting</h5>
<br>
Hail, Saint Michael the Archangel, who by your powerful intercession straighten human paths and lead the faithful toward the gates of heaven. Pray for me at all times.

<h5>Novena</h5>
<br>
Day Three:
<br>
<br>
We beseech You, Lord, for the gift of Your great mercy. Deign to send to us Your faithful messenger, Archangel Michael, to carry our humble prayers before Your throne. May he himself instruct us on what gifts we are to beg You for, and may he intercede for us, ensuring Your fatherly care for the living and eternal rest in glory for the deceased. May the Lord of His grace send Saint Michael to constantly guard our souls and bodies.
<br>
Saint Michael the Archangel, powerful patron of our basilica, we unite with you in thanksgiving and praise of God for the gift of 550 years of presence and zealous ministry of the Pauline Order at Skałka in Kraków. We express our gratitude for our noble founder, Father Jan Długosz, and for all the benefactors who throughout the centuries supported the Fathers and Brothers in their holy mission. We humbly ask you that the fruits of this distinguished Jubilee Year may bring us all the grace of an authentic and zealous life according to the teachings of the Holy Gospel.
<br>
Through the powerful intercession of Saint Michael the Archangel and the heavenly choir of Thrones, may the Lord pour into our hearts a spirit of true, sincere, and deep humility. Amen.

<h5>Pope Leo XIII Prayer to St. Michael the Archangel</h5>
<br>
Saint Michael the Archangel, defend us in battle, be our protection against the wickedness and snares of the devil; may God rebuke him, we humbly pray; and do thou, O Prince of the heavenly host, by the power of God, cast into hell Satan and all the evil spirits who prowl through the world seeking the ruin of souls. Amen.
