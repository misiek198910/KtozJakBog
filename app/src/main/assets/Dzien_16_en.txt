<h5>Day 16. The Greatness of Saint Michael the Archangel in Strengthening Human Souls</h5>
<br>
The soul of a just person not infrequently faces internal crises and discouragement. There are times when its fervor wanes, daily exercises in virtues die down, and good deeds recede into the background. In such moments, Saint Michael the Archangel hastens to help, to lift the soul from this spiritual paralysis and chaos. He pours comfort into the fearful heart and—as Saint Bernard points out—supports it in action, removing obstacles from the path and freeing it from paralyzing fear. He acts in a similar way as he did toward the prophet Elijah, who, fleeing from the persecutions of Jezebel, collapsed from lack of strength and, in deep exhaustion, begged God for death. It was then that Archangel Michael appeared to him, comforted him, and offered him food, saying: "Arise and eat, for you have a long journey ahead of you." Thanks to this heavenly strengthening, the prophet gained the strength to continue his journey all the way to the holy mountain of Horeb.
<br>
In the very same way, the holy Archangel addresses every discouraged soul, repeating: Arise, nourish yourself with God's grace, for a long and important journey awaits you. Rise to face adversities, draw strength from the sacraments, and walk the path of God's law with renewed zeal until you attain the longed-for perfection.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer</h5>
<br>
O glorious Prince of love, Saint Michael the Archangel! With deep humility, I beg you for your help and intercession. Deign to calm all fear in my restless heart and, with your powerful hand, establish my wavering steps on the path of God's commandments. If you surround me with your heavenly protection, O great Archangel, I shall be able to become a zealous and faithful servant of my Lord. When you support me, my soul will be filled with courage capable of overcoming every difficulty and temptation standing on the path to salvation. Be my shield, so that my heart may love God completely and rejoice in Him for all eternity. Amen.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Greeting</h5>
<br>
Hail, Saint Michael the Archangel, who bring comfort and fortitude to hearts gripped by fear. Remember me and intercede for me at every moment of my earthly journey.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Task</h5>
<br>
Think today of those who are lonely, isolated from the world (e.g., in hospitals, care homes, or prisons) and support them with prayer, a kind word, or a concrete form of help, bringing them hope.

<h5>Prayer to the Guardian Angel</h5>
<br>
Angel of God, my guardian dear, to whom God's love commits me here. Ever this day be at my side, to light and guard, to rule and guide. Amen.
