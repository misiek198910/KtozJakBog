<h5>Day 27. The Greatness of Saint Michael the Archangel in Protection over the Dying</h5>
<br>
Unrivaled and full of tenderness is the love of the heavenly Prince with which he hastens to the aid of his faithful devotees, especially at the dramatic moment of their transition into eternity. For the Creator has endowed him with a special authority over souls leaving this temporal world. The Holy Church venerates his intercession primarily on May 8th and September 29th—in spring and in autumn. As Saint Bonaventure deeply explains, this deliberate comparison of the seasons symbolizes the righteousness of the Archangel's protection, which shields man both in the full bloom of earthly life and in its autumnal twilight, at the hour of death. Saint Pantaleon indicates that the heavenly Leader visits the bedsides of the sick at moments when human efforts fail, loved ones become helpless, and earthly riches lose any value. It is then that Saint Michael arrives to pour in supernatural fortitude, to comfort, and to sustain his faithful servants.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer</h5>
<br>
O glorious and most illustrious Prince of Heaven, Saint Michael the Archangel! Come to my powerful rescue in this most difficult and final hour of my death. Deign to surround me with your presence in the last moments of my earthly existence and shield me in this final battle against the enemy of salvation, so that my soul, cleansed and strengthened, may safely stand before the just Tribunal of God. Amen.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Greeting</h5>
<br>
I greet you, Saint Michael the Archangel, who by the will of the Most High have been established as the heavenly Guardian of human souls. Deign in the hour of transition to lovingly receive my soul as well.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Task</h5>
<br>
With deep reverence, kiss the wounds of Jesus Christ on the image of the Holy Cross three times today, pronouncing with sincere devotion the words: "Into Your hands, O Lord, I commend my spirit."
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer to the Guardian Angel</h5>
<br>
Angel of God, my guardian dear, to whom God's love commits me here. Ever this day be at my side, to light and guard, to rule and guide. Amen.
<br>
<h5>Novena</h5>
<br>
Day Eight:
<br>
<br>
We beseech You, Lord, for the gift of Your great mercy. Deign to send to us Your faithful messenger, Archangel Michael, to carry our humble prayers before Your throne. May he himself instruct us on what gifts we are to beg You for, and may he intercede for us, ensuring Your fatherly care for the living and eternal rest in glory for the deceased. May the Lord of His grace send Saint Michael to constantly guard our souls and bodies.
<br>
Saint Michael the Archangel, powerful patron of our basilica, we unite with you in thanksgiving and praise of God for the gift of 550 years of presence and zealous ministry of the Pauline Order at Skałka in Kraków. We express our gratitude for our noble founder, Father Jan Długosz, and for all the benefactors who throughout the centuries supported the Fathers and Brothers in their holy mission. We humbly ask you that the fruits of this distinguished Jubilee Year may bring us all the grace of an authentic and zealous life according to the teachings of the Holy Gospel.
<br>
Through the powerful intercession of Saint Michael the Archangel and the heavenly choir of Archangels, may the Lord grant us the grace of steadfast perseverance in the Catholic faith and in the zealous performance of good deeds. Amen.
