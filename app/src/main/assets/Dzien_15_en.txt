<h5>Day 15. The Greatness of Saint Michael the Archangel in Love for the Just</h5>
<br>
Saint Michael the Archangel surrounds with a special and profound love the just souls who are still pilgrimming upon the earth. He constantly watches over their steps, enlightening their minds with Divine light and snatching them from the dangerous state of spiritual slumber. Just as in the biblical story, when the Angel of the Lord appeared to Saint Peter imprisoned by Herod, breaking his chains and leading him to freedom, so too does Archangel Michael surround with his powerful care all those who persevere in fervent prayer. He brings them inner inspirations, helps them perceive the subtle traps of spiritual indifference, pours a refreshing fear of God into their hearts, and helps them overcome all negligence on the path toward perfection, enabling them to do authentic good.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer</h5>
<br>
O Saint Michael the Archangel, I fly to your defense as one whose heart is so often host to spiritual laziness and lukewarmness. I beg you for the flame of your fervent and pure love. Grant, powerful Prince of Goodness, that my soul may be cleansed from all indifference, and let my mind shine with holy inspirations. Support me, so that I may with determination reject every sin and cling with all my strength to the Christian virtues, which constitute the only, lasting, and true wealth of man. Amen.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Greeting</h5>
<br>
Hail, Saint Michael the Archangel, heavenly Messenger bringing spiritual support and strengthening to the souls of the just. Rescue me and guard me in every minute of my path.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Task</h5>
<br>
Approach the Sacrament of the Eucharist today with a pure and prepared heart and receive Holy Communion, which is the most splendid Food giving strength for constant growth in faith and love.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer to the Guardian Angel</h5>
<br>
Angel of God, my guardian dear, to whom God's love commits me here. Ever this day be at my side, to light and guard, to rule and guide. Amen.
