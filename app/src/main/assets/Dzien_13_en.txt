<h5>Day 13. The Greatness of Saint Michael the Archangel in the Defense of the Angelic Hosts</h5>
<br>
Saint Michael the Archangel, standing as a shield and Defender of the pure spirits, confirmed them in boundless fidelity and love for God, securing their eternal happiness in the heavens. How powerful was the might carried within the words he uttered then: "Who is like God?". Let us try to imagine that dramatic moment of clash in the heavenly kingdom. Lucifer, blinded by pride and audaciously desiring to equal himself with the Creator, managed to draw one-third of the heavenly hosts with him, who along with him rebelled against the eternal Majesty, striking at His holy throne.
<br>
How great a number of additional angels would have succumbed to these subtle persuasions had it not been for the firm and vehement reaction of Saint Michael, who uttered his monumental cry: "Who is like God!". These words were a powerful warning to the entire heaven: Watch and do not allow yourselves to be deceived by this proud dragon! No creature is able to match God, who called us into being. Who is like God? He alone is the fullness of unfathomable perfection and the inexhaustible source of all joy. Without His love, we all are merely nothingness.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer</h5>
<br>
Invincible Defender of the angelic hosts, glorious Michael the Archangel! I fly to your protective wings, as I constantly experience the subtle attacks and snares of the enemy of my soul. A heavy and exhausting battle for fidelity to God is being waged in my heart. However, I deeply believe that the power of your arm and your heavenly shield are infinitely stronger than the forces of darkness. With full confidence, I take refuge under your defense, looking forward to victory over evil. Guard me at all times and on every path of my life, so that I may safely reach the port of salvation. Amen.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Greeting</h5>
<br>
Hail, Saint Michael the Archangel, along with all the faithful angels of peace. Do not cease day and night in repelling the attacks of the evil spirit and be my steadfast protection.
