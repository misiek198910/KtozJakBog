<h5>Day 30. The Greatness of Saint Michael the Archangel in Might During the Last Judgment</h5>
<br>
It is precisely to the glorious and most illustrious Saint Michael the Archangel that, during the Last Judgment, the solemn announcement to the just of the saving verdict of eternal glory will belong. It is he who will reveal to the elect the final decree of the Divine Judge: "Come, you who are blessed by my Father; take your inheritance, the kingdom prepared for you since the creation of the world!". As Origen beautifully notes, Archangel Michael will act as the noble vicar of the Lord's vineyard, whose task and privilege it will become to hand to the saints their well-deserved reward for their good deeds and fidelity shown during life.
<br>
Do not fear, therefore, believer, for Saint Michael is established as a special patron and defender of all who seek God. Serve him with devotion, love him, and surround him with sincere reverence, and on the final day, he will arrive with joy to adorn your soul, with the consent of the Most High, with the unfading crown of glory.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer</h5>
<br>
O most glorious Prince of the heavenly hosts! Through your unfathomable, angelic love toward men, deign to constantly stand by my side and make me worthy of your special, heavenly protection. Be my steadfast defender, advocate, and source of deep consolation on that solemn day of Judgment before the countenance of the just God. Lead my soul straight toward the heights of heaven, so that together with you I may endlessly marvel at the holy presence of my Creator. Amen.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Greeting</h5>
<br>
Hail, Saint Michael the Archangel, most noble worker in the vineyard of the Lord. Obtain for me from the Lord the grace that my soul may be included in the glorious choir of the just in the Kingdom of Heaven.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Task</h5>
<br>
Open your heart today to the needs of others and, according to your possibilities, support the poor or needy with a sincere, selfless alms, done out of love for God.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer to the Guardian Angel</h5>
<br>
Angel of God, my guardian dear, to whom God's love commits me here. Ever this day be at my side, to light and guard, to rule and guide. Amen.
