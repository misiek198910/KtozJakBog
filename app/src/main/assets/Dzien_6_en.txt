<h5>Day 6. The Greatness of Saint Michael the Archangel in the Virtue of Hope</h5>
<br>
Consider how steadfast and pure was the hope of Saint Michael the Archangel, constituting the complete opposite of the presumption and pride of Lucifer. The rebellious leader, blinded by the brilliance of his own countless gifts of nature, instead of treating them as a path leading to eternal union with the Creator, began to worship himself. He behaved as if his own essence were the source of happiness, rejecting the truth that only in God does a creature find its fulfillment.
<br>
When this false desire for independence was born in Lucifer's heart, he immediately realized the illusion of his pursuits, and his magnificent attributes became the cause of his eternal ruin. At that dramatic moment of trial, the powerful hope of Saint Michael triumphed. Seeing the terrifying downfall of such a perfect spirit, the Archangel did not succumb to fear or doubt, but united himself even more strongly with the Lord, repeating after the inspired Psalmist: "But for me it is good to be near God." He placed his entire trust in the Creator and, standing courageously against the rebellious hosts, proclaimed: No creature can attain happiness apart from God. Everything I possess and all that I am is a gift of the supernatural goodness of the Father, who opens before me the gates of eternal glory. O how splendid and pure is the hope of Saint Michael!
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer</h5>
<br>
Lord, God full of mercy, You are my only and ultimate support! In You, who in Your infinite love have promised me the inheritance of the eternal kingdom, I place all my longings and aspirations. The holy wounds of my Savior, Jesus Court, are for me the source of spiritual healing and peace. And you, glorious Saint Michael the Archangel, be my advocate before the throne of the Most High and obtain for me the grace necessary to attain eternal joy in heaven. Cleanse my heart from all conceit, self-will, and pride, so that I may be able to trust boundlessly in the Divine promise alone and, with your help, attain salvation. Amen.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Greeting</h5>
<br>
Hail, Saint Michael the Archangel, reliable help and pillar of all who with full confidence place their hope in the wisdom and love of God.
<br>

<h5>Task</h5>
<br>
Awaken within yourself a deep trust and recite the Act of Hope nine times, uniting yourself spiritually with the nine choirs of angels, who admire in awe the boundless entrustment of Saint Michael the Archangel.
