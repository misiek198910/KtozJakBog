<h5>Day 26. The Greatness of Saint Michael the Archangel in Sealing the Souls of the Elect</h5>
<br>
Saint Michael the Archangel loves his devotees with such a great and fervent love that he constantly and devotedly strives for their destination to eternal happiness in the heavens. In the liturgy and tradition of the Church, he is often called the minister of salvation and the giver of spiritual health, because he not only reverently carries before us the Holy Cross—the sign of our redemption and rescue—but also seals the souls of the just, protecting them from ruin. In such a wondrous way, Saint John the Apostle presents him in the Book of Revelation, describing a powerful angel coming from the rising of the sun, holding in his hand the intact seal of the living God.
<br>
This glorious Leader cries out with power to the four angels to whom authority over the elements was given, commanding them to hold back the destructive forces on the earth and sea. He asks them not to cause any harm to the world until he marks the foreheads of all the faithful servants of God. Just as in the final times the deceiver will attempt to provide his followers with the mark of damnation, so the glorious Archangel Michael lovingly seals those chosen for the glory of heaven. Pray fervently today that he may obtain this indispensable sign of fidelity and eternal rescue for your soul.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer</h5>
<br>
O powerful Prince of Heaven, Saint Michael the Archangel! With deep reverence, I adore you and praise the Lord for the immeasurable power and gifts with which He adorned your angelic nature. I humbly beg you, deign in your goodness to accept me into the ranks of your faithful servants and charges. Be my advocate before the throne of the Merciful Father and obtain for me the grace to be permanently rooted in His love. Surround me with your constant protection, multiply my strength in daily struggles with temptations, so that, supported by your arm, I may one day rejoice with you in beholding God in the kingdom of light. Amen.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Greeting</h5>
<br>
I greet you, Saint Michael the Archangel, and I ask: obtain for me from the Lord the grace that my soul may be forever marked with the holy seal of fidelity and belonging to the ranks of His elect.
<br>

<h5>Task</h5>
<br>
Watch your words carefully today and under no circumstances speak ill of your neighbors. If you happen to fail this rule, immediately repair this mistake with a kind word, a prayer, or an act of kindness toward that person.

<h5>Prayer to the Guardian Angel</h5>
<br>
Angel of God, my guardian dear, to whom God's love commits me here. Ever this day be at my side, to light and guard, to rule and guide. Amen.
<br>
<h5>Novena</h5>
<br>
Day Seven:
<br>
<br>
We beseech You, Lord, for the gift of Your great mercy. Deign to send to us Your faithful messenger, Archangel Michael, to carry our humble prayers before Your throne. May he himself instruct us on what gifts we are to beg You for, and may he intercede for us, ensuring Your fatherly care for the living and eternal rest in glory for the deceased. May the Lord of His grace send Saint Michael to constantly guard our souls and bodies.
<br>
Saint Michael the Archangel, powerful patron of our basilica, we unite with you in thanksgiving and praise of God for the gift of 550 years of presence and zealous ministry of the Pauline Order at Skałka in Kraków. We express our gratitude for our noble founder, Father Jan Długosz, and for all the benefactors who throughout the centuries supported the Fathers and Brothers in their holy mission. We humbly ask you that the fruits of this distinguished Jubilee Year may bring us all the grace of an authentic and zealous life according to the teachings of the Holy Gospel.
<br>
Through the powerful intercession of Saint Michael the Archangel and the heavenly choir of Principalities, may the Lord fill our hearts with a spirit of true, sincere, and joyful obedience to the Divine will. Amen.

<h5>Pope Leo XIII Prayer to St. Michael the Archangel</h5>
<br>
Saint Michael the Archangel, defend us in battle, be our protection against the wickedness and snares of the devil; may God rebuke him, we humbly pray; and do thou, O Prince of the heavenly host, by the power of God, cast into hell Satan and all the evil spirits who prowl through the world seeking the ruin of souls. Amen.
