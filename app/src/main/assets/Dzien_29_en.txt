<h5>Day 29. The Greatness of Saint Michael the Archangel in Might Against the Antichrist</h5>
<br>
How unfathomable is the power of Saint Michael the Archangel and how splendid are the fruits of his heavenly triumphs! Fly to his powerful protection and seek a safe refuge under the wings of this courageous Commander. Do not cease in fervent prayer, asking him for deliverance from the snares and temptations of modern antichrists, who are not lacking in these current times full of iniquity and confusion. Even among people who call themselves Christians, dangerous errors are spreading today, striking directly at the purity of the Gospel and the eternal teaching of the Church. How much distortion of the truth and deception of imprudent souls is around us! How many people by scandalous example and false witness drag others onto the path of eternal ruin!
<br>
Turn with deep faith to the heavenly Warrior and beg him not to cease enlightening your mind with Divine light, protecting you from any error in faith. May he himself guard your heart, so that despite the widespread corruption and wickedness of this world, it may always remain unblemished, pure, and boundlessly in love with God. Ask him earnestly to constantly support the Church of Jesus Christ in achieving the ultimate victory over the evil that attempts to assert itself in the world.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer</h5>
<br>
O victorious Warrior of Heaven, Saint Michael the Archangel! How very much the intervention of your powerful, just arm is needed today in defense of the Holy Church, which Satan—using his earthly servants who are overt antichrists—does not cease to despise, curse, and ruthlessly persecute. To you, O undaunted Archangel, the guard over the Bark of Peter has been entrusted. Increase our faith, renew within our hearts an unwavering hope, and perfect our love, so that with all supernatural power we may be able to oppose the enemies of Jesus Christ and always triumph in the confession of the holy faith. Amen.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Greeting</h5>
<br>
Hail, Saint Michael the Archangel, noble Messenger of the Lord of Hosts and steadfast Defender of the Christian faith. Shield me from all enemies of the holy truth.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Task</h5>
<br>
Undertake a decisive battle today against your own pride and self-love, applying small, discreet penitential practices—both external (e.g., a moment of giving up comfort) and internal (e.g., refraining from a malicious retort or patiently bearing others' faults).
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer to the Guardian Angel</h5>
<br>
Angel of God, my guardian dear, to whom God's love commits me here. Ever this day be at my side, to light and guard, to rule and guide. Amen.
