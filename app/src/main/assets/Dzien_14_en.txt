<h5>Day 14. The Greatness of Saint Michael the Archangel in Love for Mankind</h5>
<br>
The unfathomable devotion with which Saint Michael the Archangel surrounds every human being has its source in his boundless and pure love for God Himself. It is precisely from love for the Creator that authentic care for one's neighbor is born, who carries within themselves the living image and likeness of the Most High. The more strongly a heart unites with the Lord, the greater and deeper is the love it bestows upon another person. While the fallen Lucifer became a fierce enemy of humanity because he completely uprooted the love of God from within himself, Saint Michael, of all the heavenly spirits, loves and supports man the most in his earthly struggles.
<br>
Consider today whether your own heart is truly filled with sincere love for the people you meet. Do you pass indifferently by the weeping and cries of those who are experiencing affliction? Do you perceive the real needs of the poor and abandoned? Do you possess sensitivity to the quiet tears of the suffering and the sick? If compassion for the pain of your neighbor is lacking in your daily life, it may mean that your own flame of love for God has begun to fade. If you loved the Lord with all your strength, the plight of another person would never be a matter of indifference to you, and bringing comfort to the tormented would become your natural need. Fly to the protection of Saint Michael, the first Seraph of love, so that he may ignite within you this holy fire that enables you to love your brothers and sisters just as Jesus Christ commanded us.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer</h5>
<br>
O most noble Prince of love, Saint Michael the Archangel! I, a weak and wretched sinner, fly with great humility to your powerful wings. Trusting boundlessly in your heavenly goodness, I beg you: ignite in my cold heart an authentic, fervent love for God and sincere compassion for every neighbor. Grant, courageous Archangel, that by imitating your devotion, I may be able to generously show mercy to those who need it, and thereby attain God's forgiveness and grace on the day of judgment. Amen.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Greeting</h5>
<br>
I greet you, Saint Michael the Archangel, who of all the heavenly spirits have loved the human race the most. Support me at every moment of my life.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Task</h5>
<br>
Visit someone today who is sick, lonely, or elderly in your surroundings, offering them your time and a kind word, or support someone with a concrete alms who is struggling with poverty.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer to the Guardian Angel</h5>
<br>
Angel of God, my guardian dear, to whom God's love commits me here. Ever this day be at my side, to light and guard, to rule and guide. Amen.
