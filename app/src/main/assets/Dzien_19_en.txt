<h5>Day 19. The Greatness of Saint Michael the Archangel in Warning Against Sin</h5>
<br>
An extraordinary testimony of the love of Saint Michael the Archangel was his care for the first sinner on earth—Adam, whom he protectively instructed after the fall and expulsion from paradise on how to shield himself from further snares of evil. In the very same way, the Archangel acts with every human being entangled in sin: he patiently teaches them to avoid occasions of downfall and warns against nourishing the heart with destructive vices. Just as in the biblical story the rescued Lot received a clear command from the messenger of heaven not to look back under any circumstances at burning Sodom, so too Saint Michael calls the sinner to a complete break from former passions. He points out how dangerous it is to attach oneself again to what once enslaved the soul. The fervent love of the heavenly Leader desires only that man sincerely reconcile with the Father and never again separate himself from His life-giving grace.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Prayer</h5>
<br>
O great Prince of love and mercy, Saint Michael the Archangel! I stand before your countenance, fully aware of my immense instability and how easily and thoughtlessly I succumb to temptations. From the bottom of my heart, I call for your powerful assistance. Obtain for me from the Lord the grace and strength never to return to my past sins, and grant that my feet may walk firmly upon the path of the holy commandments. Do not deny me your protection in moments of spiritual combat—protect me and lead me until that joyful day when I shall stand secure at your feet in the heavens, to praise the Majesty of God together with you for all eternity. Amen.
<br>
<br>
<h5>Greeting</h5>
<br>
Hail, Saint Michael the Archangel, steadfast defender and shield of all who place their entire trust in the wisdom and mercy of the Lord. Come to my rescue in every hour of trial.
